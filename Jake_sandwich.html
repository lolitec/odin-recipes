Here you will learn to make Jake´s Perfect Sandwich from "Adventure Time"

Ingredients

For sous vide steak

  • 8 oz prime steak
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 1-2 teaspoons salt
  • 1-2 teaspoons pepper

For "Bird on Window"

  • 1 cornish hen (thawed if frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 egg hard boiled

Other sandwich ingredients

  • 1 loaf sourdough or french bread
  • 4 oz cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill
  • 4 bread and butter pickle slices
  • 2 slices roma tomato
  • 5-6 slices english cucumber
  • 2 slices yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 1 lobster (optional)

Instructions

For sous vide steak

  1. Prep a water bath with an immersion circulator to 135°F. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper. Place the steak into a vacuum-seal bag with rosemary and thyme sprigs, then seal the bag.
  2. Transfer the bag to the water bath and cook for 30 minutes or while preparing the rest of the ingredients. When ready, remove from the water bath and allow the steak to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

For air fryer cornish hens

  1. Preheat the air fryer for 5 minutes at 350°F. Use paper towels to dry the hen thoroughly both outside and inside the chest cavity. Place any remaining herbs from the steak inside the hen, if available.
  2. Coat the hen on all sides with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Place it in the air fryer basket and cook at 375°F for 15-20 minutes. Let it rest in the basket for 10 minutes before carving. Carve the breasts and reserve the rest for another use.

Misc. ingredients

  1. Boil the egg until hard-boiled, about 8-10 minutes in boiling water.
  2. Fry the bacon strips until crispy, about 5-8 minutes in a skillet.

Assembling the sandwich

  1. Slice the bread down the middle lengthwise. Use a brulee torch to toast the inside surfaces until crispy. Then spread cream cheese evenly over each half.
  2. Layer the sandwich with pickle slices, dill, cucumber, tomato, onions, chicken breasts, sliced hard-boiled egg, and slices of sous vide steak. Top with bacon strips and cooked lobster if using.
  3. Place on the top of the sandwich and enjoy before the Magic Man can get his grabby little hands on your woman!
